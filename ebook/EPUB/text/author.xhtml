Christine Love is the co-founder of Love Conquers All Games, a writer and game designer who makes narrative games about the only thing she knows: messy queer women. Her works cover a wide range of stories, from action-packed adventures to thoughtful epistolary tragedies. Her most recent game is the road trip adventure Get in the Car, Loser!, and her previous erotic romantic comedy Ladykiller in a Bind won the IGF 2017 award for Excellence in Narrative.
